Preliminary Design Review

02/21

DJS Arya secured the 2nd rank in the world for our Preliminary Design Review (PDR) round in the International CanSat Competition 2020 - 2021. The PDR is the first round of the competition in which we secured a score of 99.6269 %. Our team compiled all our work into a report that we presented to our judges at an international level. Every department contributed to make sure that our report was flawless and presented to the best of our abilities. “Well begun is half done.” This was the perfect start to our campaign which has allowed us to work harder for the upcoming Critical Design Review (CDR) round. We have also begun testing our prototype CanSat for the launch round of the competition.

Critical Design Review

04/21

DJS Arya secured the 1st rank in India and the 3rd rank in the World, overall after our Critical Design Review (CDR) round in the International CanSat Competition 2020 - 2021. We have secured an average score of 99.3861 % after both our reviews. Our team compiled all our work and efforts into the final report that was presented to the judges at an international level. Every department worked hard to ensure that our CanSat as well as our final report was flawless and presented to the best of our abilities. Having completed both our review rounds we are all set to conduct our remaining set of tests for the competition. These include the Environmental Test and the live demonstration of our CanSat.

Environmental Tests

05/21

Environmental test were performed to check the functionality of the CanSat. They are subcategorized into four main tests: The Drop test is designed to verify that the container parachute and attachment point survive the violent deployment from the rocket payloads section. The Vibration test is is designed to verify the mounting integrity of all components, mounting connections, structural integrity, and battery connections. The Vacuum test is designed to verify deployment operation of the payload(s). The Thermal test is carried out to verify the CanSat and container can operate in a hot environment. Our team successfully carried out the above mentioned tests, thus ensuring proper functioning of the CanSat.
